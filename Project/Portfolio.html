Welcome to My Portfolio

In this portfolio, I share my life journey — from my early beginnings and the challenges I faced growing up, to where I am today, working as a Logistics Coordinator at Acadian Driveaway. While building my career in logistics, I also discovered a deep passion for programming. What started as simple curiosity grew into a genuine love for problem-solving and creating through code. This portfolio highlights the path I’ve taken, the skills I’ve developed, and the new direction I’m excited to explore in tech
